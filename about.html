Our Story

Sekgwari Primary School, nestled in the heart of Gamatlala, has been a cornerstone of education in our community for over three decades. Since our establishment in 1990, we have been committed to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders through quality education and strong values.

Our school combines traditional values with modern teaching methods, ensuring our learners receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for future success. We take pride in our diverse and inclusive learning environment where every child is valued and supported.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary school that nurtures academic excellence, personal growth, and community values, while preparing our learners for a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers learners with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for their future success while preserving our cultural heritage and inspiring lifelong learning.
