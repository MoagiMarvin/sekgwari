Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll my child?

Please contact our admissions office to schedule a visit and receive enrollment information. We'll guide you through the entire process.

What grades do you offer?

We offer primary education from Grade R (Reception Year) through Grade 7, providing a complete foundation for secondary education.

Do you offer transportation?

Please contact our office for information about transportation options available for students in your area.

What are your class sizes?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure individual attention for each learner, typically with a student-teacher ratio that promotes effective learning.
